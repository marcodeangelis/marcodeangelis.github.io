Marco De Angelis

I am a postdoctoral research associate at the University of Liverpool (UoL) since May 2018. During my post I will be developing software for the efficient error and uncertainty propagation through computational codes. Previously, I was the manager of the doctoral training centre at the Institute for Risk and Uncertainty of the UoL for over two years. I hold a PhD in risk and uncertainty in engineering from UoL, an MSc cum laude in engineering for the mitigation of natural risks and a Bsc cum laude in structural engineering from the University of Rome, Roma Tre.
