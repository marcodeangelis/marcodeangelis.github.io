Marco De Angelis

Postdoc in engineering

I'm a research associate at the University of Liverpool. My research focuses on theoretical and practical aspects of computing with uncertainty. My areas of interest are: (1) rigorous inference from small-sized and uncertain data; (2) propagation of uncertainty through complex computations; (3) code optimization. I am also interested in: rigorous computing, computer-aided proof, modelling of time-history data, neural differential equations, web-based research software, mathematics-based artwork. My research is helping casting more light on: (*) digital twins technology, (*) hard-to-diagnose diseases, (*) robust and explainable AI, (*) earthquake simulation. I hold a PhD in engineering from the University of Liverpool.
